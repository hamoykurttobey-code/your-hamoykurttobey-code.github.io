My Film TV Shows

Series I keep coming back to — K-Dramas, romance teleseryes, and action-packed shows.

K-Drama

Korean dramas that completely captured my heart

Twenty-Five Twenty-One
2022  |  tvN  |  16 Episodes
Director: Jung Ji-hyun
Stars: Kim Tae-ri, Nam Joo-hyuk

During the 1998 financial crisis, a fencer and a young man pursuing his dreams form an unforgettable bond — a bittersweet, beautifully written coming-of-age romance.

Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-joo
2016–2017  |  MBC  |  16 Episodes
Director: Oh Hyun-jong
Stars: Lee Sung-kyung, Nam Joo-hyuk

A talented weightlifter and a swimmer rediscover their childhood friendship while chasing their athletic dreams — a warm, funny, and deeply heartfelt romance.

Crash Landing on You
2019–2020  |  tvN  |  16 Episodes
Director: Lee Jeong-hyo
Stars: Son Ye-jin, Hyun Bin

A South Korean heiress accidentally paraglides into North Korea and falls in love with an elite army officer — a stunning romance across divided borders.

Romance

Teleseryes and shows that hit close to the heart

On the Wings of Love
2015–2016  |  ABS-CBN  |  Teleserye
Director: Antoinette Jadaone
Stars: James Reid, Nadine Lustre

Two strangers enter a marriage of convenience in the US, only to fall genuinely in love — a beloved Filipino teleserye that swept the nation.

Dolce Amore
2016  |  ABS-CBN  |  Teleserye
Stars: Enrique Gil, Liza Soberano

A Filipino-Italian love story set in the beautiful backdrop of Italy — a sweet and charming teleserye full of passion and heartfelt moments.

The World Between Us
2021  |  GMA  |  Teleserye
Stars: Alden Richards, Jasmine Curtis-Smith

A love story between two people from completely different worlds who find each other against all odds — a heartwarming and emotional Filipino drama.

Action

High-octane series that keep me hooked every episode

The Flash
2014–2023  |  The CW  |  9 Seasons
Creator: Greg Berlanti
Stars: Grant Gustin, Candice Patton, Tom Cavanagh

A forensic scientist struck by lightning gains superhuman speed and becomes The Flash — a fun, emotional, and action-packed superhero series.

Arrow
2012–2020  |  The CW  |  8 Seasons
Creator: Greg Berlanti
Stars: Stephen Amell, Emily Bett Rickards, David Ramsey

A billionaire returns from a deserted island as a hooded vigilante to save his city — the gritty DC series that launched the entire Arrowverse.

Cobra Kai
2018–2025  |  Netflix  |  6 Seasons
Creators: Josh Heald, Jon Hurwitz, Hayden Schlossberg
Stars: Ralph Macchio, William Zabka

Decades after the Karate Kid tournament, rivals reconnect as karate teachers with competing philosophies — a brilliant, action-packed continuation of a classic.
