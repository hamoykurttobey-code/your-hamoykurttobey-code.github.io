My Film Movies

My personal collection of favorite films — handpicked across comedy, romance, and action.

Comedy

Movies that never fail to make me laugh

The Mask
1994  |  PG-13  |  1h 41m
Director: Chuck Russell
Stars: Jim Carrey, Cameron Diaz, Peter Greene

A mild-mannered bank clerk discovers an ancient mask that transforms him into a wild, cartoonish superhero — hilarious chaos follows.

Ang Pangarap Kong Holdap
2022  |  R-13  |  1h 40m
Director: Roman Perez Jr.
Stars: Joshua Garcia, Kokoy de Santos, Vance Larena

Three broke best friends hatch a plan to rob a bank to solve their problems — a hilarious Filipino comedy about friendship, dreams, and going all-in.

Hancock
2008  |  PG-13  |  1h 32m
Director: Peter Berg
Stars: Will Smith, Charlize Theron, Jason Bateman

A superhero with a bad attitude and a drinking problem reluctantly agrees to a PR makeover — a funny and surprisingly emotional ride.

Romance

Love stories that hit different

Till I Met You
2016  |  ABS-CBN  |  Teleserye
Director: Cathy Garcia-Molina
Stars: James Reid, Nadine Lustre

Two strangers with complicated pasts find unexpected love in Greece — a sweeping, romantic teleserye that captured all of the Philippines.

Titanic
1997  |  PG-13  |  3h 14m
Director: James Cameron
Stars: Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet

A poor artist and a wealthy socialite fall deeply in love aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic — one of the greatest love stories ever told.

The Hows of Us
2018  |  PG  |  2h 4m
Director: Cathy Garcia-Molina
Stars: Kathryn Bernardo, Daniel Padilla

Two young lovers navigate the highs and lows of a long-term relationship — a raw, honest, and deeply moving Filipino romance.

Action

High-energy films that keep me at the edge of my seat

Bad Boys
1995  |  R  |  1h 59m
Director: Michael Bay
Stars: Will Smith, Martin Lawrence

Two Miami detectives must protect a witness while investigating a drug heist — an explosive, hilarious buddy cop action classic.

John Wick
2014  |  R  |  1h 41m
Director: Chad Stahelski
Stars: Keanu Reeves, Michael Nyqvist, Alfie Allen

A retired hitman unleashes vengeance after criminals kill his dog — a gift from his late wife. A masterpiece of modern action cinema.

Avengers: Endgame
2019  |  PG-13  |  3h 1m
Directors: Anthony & Joe Russo
Stars: Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Scarlett Johansson

The surviving Avengers assemble one last time to reverse Thanos’ devastation — the ultimate superhero epic and an emotional farewell to an era.
