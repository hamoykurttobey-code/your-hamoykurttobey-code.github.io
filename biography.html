The Story of My Early Years: Kurt Tobey Hamoy

Click image to view larger

Childhood Years

1 I was born in Quezon City as the third child in our family. 2 We were two boys and two girl growing up together. 3 Life was financially difficult during my childhood. 4 My parents became parents at a young age. 5 My father dreamed of becoming a policeman. 6 He stopped studying to support our family. 7 My mother stayed home to take care of us. 8 Despite hardships, my parents stayed united. 9 I witnessed many sacrifices from them. 10 Responsibility was taught to me early. 11 Arguments happened but forgiveness followed. 12 These moments taught me family values. 13 I trained in Taekwondo as a child. 14 Training taught me discipline and patience. 15 Pain helped build my strength. 16 I learned endurance at a young age. 17 My siblings and I shared a strong bond. 18 I learned to value small victories. 19 Challenges helped shape my character. 20 Childhood formed the foundation of who I am.

Teenage Years

1 My teenage years were full of learning experiences. 2 I studied at Sta. Elena High School. 3 I later transferred to OLOPSC. 4 These years tested my maturity. 5 I faced challenges that shaped my attitude. 6 I learned accountability from my mistakes. 7 Responsibility became important to me. 8 I focused more on my studies. 9 Sports became a big part of my life. 10 Basketball and Taekwondo taught discipline. 11 I joined school competitions. 12 Recognition motivated me to improve. 13 Friendships grew stronger. 14 I learned teamwork and trust. 15 Balancing school and friends was challenging. 16 Failures helped me grow emotionally. 17 I started setting personal goals. 18 My confidence slowly improved. 19 Hard work became my habit. 20 My teenage years shaped my dreams.

College Years

1 College marked a new chapter in my life. 2 I pursued Information Technology. 3 I enrolled at the University of the East. 4 Independence was both exciting and scary. 5 I learned time management. 6 Academic work became harder. 7 Some subjects tested my confidence. 8 I learned to face challenges bravely. 9 College improved my critical thinking. 10 Group work improved communication. 11 Teamwork became important. 12 I discovered interest in technology. 13 My passion for IT grew. 14 Adjusting to college life was challenging. 15 I learned from my decisions. 16 Discipline became part of my routine. 17 Balancing studies and hobbies was difficult. 18 Friends helped motivate me. 19 Challenges became learning experiences. 20 College shaped me into a responsible person.
